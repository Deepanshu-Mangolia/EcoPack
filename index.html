Eco Friendly Packaging

Packaging supports every industry, from food delivery to medical supplies. But conventional materials like polyethylene and plastic are hard to recycle and harmful to the environment. We offer a thoughtfully curated range of eco-friendly alternatives that are affordable, durable, and responsibly made, including plantable packaging, rice husk containers, and more.

Why Plastic?

Plastic is widely used because it is easy to source and low cost, making it the default choice for many businesses.

Cons

  • Environmental Degradation
  • Low Melting Point
  • Threat to marine life
  • One of the lead cause to global warming

Eco friendly packaging

Eco-friendly packaging is easy to recycle, safe for people and the planet, and made from recycled or renewable materials. It minimizes energy use and protects natural resources.

Applications

Because it is biodegradable and safe, eco packaging works across many uses: food delivery, gift packaging, utensils, and more.

The Era of Eco-Friendly Packaging

Customizable paper bag

A customizable paper bag made from recycled materials, designed to carry more than just your products. These logo-printed bags are recyclable, biodegradable, and reusable, offering an elegant alternative to plastic. Made with post-consumer paper, they deliver a premium feel while reducing your footprint.

Transparent meal box

A transparent meal box that showcases your food while supporting sustainability. Crafted from recyclable or compostable materials like PET or PLA, it keeps meals fresh, visible, and secure. Durable, leak-resistant, and customizable for takeaway, meal prep, or corporate lunches.

Reusable cloth bag

A reusable cloth bag crafted from natural, biodegradable fabrics like cotton or jute. Designed for repeated use, it reduces waste while offering a stylish, durable option for shopping, gifting, or daily carry.

Customizable paper cup

A customizable paper cup made from responsibly sourced, food-grade paper with optional compostable or recyclable linings. Ideal for hot or cold drinks, with a durable, leak-resistant build and plenty of space for your branding.

PethaPack (made from petha waste)

A packaging solution crafted from petha waste that transforms food by-products into biodegradable, durable boxes. Lightweight, compostable, and customizable, PethaPack supports circular economy practices and reduces landfill impact.
